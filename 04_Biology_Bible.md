# 04 --- Biology Bible

**Version:** 1.0\
**Status:** Authoritative Reference\
**Tier:** Biology

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Purpose

The Biology Bible defines the biological rules that govern life in
Chlora.

It establishes the principles that all organisms, ecosystems, and
symbiotic relationships must follow. It defines **how biology works**,
not every organism that exists.

Individual species belong in the Bestiary.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Biological Philosophy

All life in the Wilds is grounded in real biological principles before
speculative extrapolation.

Mystery should arise from incomplete scientific understanding rather
than magic.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Wilds

The Wilds are a persistent biological environment with environmental
conditions unlike those elsewhere on the planet.

These conditions permit biological processes that cannot be sustained
beyond the Edge.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Accelerated Biology

Within the Wilds:

-   Growth is accelerated.
-   Development is accelerated.
-   Ecological succession is rapid.
-   Adaptation occurs over compressed timescales.

The underlying mechanism remains unknown.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Edge

The Edge separates the Wilds from the surrounding world.

Wild organisms are adapted to the environmental conditions within the
Edge much as deep-sea organisms are adapted to high pressure.

Outside those conditions:

-   Long-term survival is limited.
-   Biological function degrades.
-   Symbiotic relationships fail.

The Edge is not a wall or force field.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Symbiosis

Symbiosis is a dominant strategy within the Wilds.

Relationships may include:

-   Mutualism
-   Commensalism
-   Parasitism

Many organisms depend upon partnerships for survival.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Chlora's Symbiont

-   A juvenile life stage of a larger biological lineage.
-   Living organism rather than technology.
-   Dormant outside favorable conditions.
-   Biologically active within suitable environments.
-   Primarily an ecological interface rather than a weapon.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Evolutionary Rules

Every future organism should:

-   Derive from real biology.
-   Occupy a believable ecological niche.
-   Exhibit evolutionary trade-offs.
-   Participate in larger ecological systems.
-   Strengthen the coherence of the ecosystem.
