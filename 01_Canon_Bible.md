# Premise

-   Humanity inhabits a mechanical Skyhook connected to the planet
    below.
-   The planet contains an ancient biological environment known as the
    Wilds.
-   Chlora forms a rare partnership with a juvenile symbiont.
-   Her partnership enables unprecedented interaction between humanity
    and the Wilds.

# Skyhook

-   Entirely human-built.
-   Entirely mechanical.
-   Extends from the planetary surface toward orbit.
-   Center of human civilization.

# Humanity

-   The Frozen provide long-term leadership.
-   Custodians maintain civilization.
-   Pickers recover material and specimens exposed by the Edge.

# Wilds

-   Ancient biological environment.
-   Inspired by real microbiology at macroscopic scale.
-   Biology proceeds at accelerated rates.
-   Organisms derive from real biology through speculative evolution.

# Edge

-   Invisible environmental transition.
-   Detectable by human instruments.
-   Advances and retreats over time.
-   Creates the Outlier.

# Outlier

-   Transitional region created by the movement of the Edge.
-   Human engineering and Wild biology coexist there.

# Symbiont

-   Living juvenile organism.
-   Not technology.
-   Dormant outside suitable conditions.
-   Ecological functions take priority over combat.

# Chlora

-   Protagonist.
-   Not chosen by destiny.
-   Distinguished by rare compatibility with the symbiont.
