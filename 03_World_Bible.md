## Purpose

The World Bible defines the physical world of Chlora.

It is the authoritative reference for geography, environments,
infrastructure, and the relationship between major locations.

Scientific mechanisms belong in the Biology Bible. Historical events
belong in the Story Bible. Only established locations and environmental
facts belong here.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# World Overview

The world of Chlora is defined by the interaction of two fundamentally
different environments:

-   The Skyhook
-   The Wilds

Between them lies the Outlier, a shifting frontier created by the
movement of the Edge.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Skyhook

The Skyhook is humanity's home and the center of civilization.

### Characteristics

-   Entirely mechanical
-   Practical aerospace engineering
-   Functional architecture
-   Built for maintenance and longevity
-   Extends from the planetary surface toward orbit

### Visual Identity

-   Straight geometry
-   Structural framing
-   Industrial materials
-   Artificial lighting
-   Visible maintenance systems

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Wilds

The Wilds are an ancient biological environment.

They are visually inspired by microscopic biology expressed at landscape
scale.

### Characteristics

-   Living environments
-   Continuous ecological activity
-   Dense biological complexity
-   Constant environmental motion
-   Accelerated biological processes (see Biology Bible)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Edge

The Edge is the invisible environmental transition surrounding the
Wilds.

It is not a physical barrier.

### Characteristics

-   Detectable by human instruments
-   Slowly advances and retreats
-   Creates the Outlier
-   Marks the transition between environmental conditions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Outlier

The Outlier is the frontier between humanity and the Wilds.

Its size changes as the Edge moves.

### Characteristics

-   Human structures intermingle with biological growth
-   Continuous maintenance
-   Scientific research
-   Salvage operations
-   Ecological transition

This is where Chlora's journey begins.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Human Presence

Human activity is concentrated around the Skyhook and the Outlier.

Major occupations include:

-   Custodians
-   Researchers
-   Maintenance crews
-   Pickers

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Environmental Progression

The player's experience moves gradually through these environments:

1.  Skyhook
2.  Lower infrastructure
3.  Outlier
4.  Edge crossing
5.  Wilds

The transition should feel ecological rather than abrupt.
