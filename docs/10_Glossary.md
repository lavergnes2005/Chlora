# CHLORA PROJECT

# 10 --- Glossary

**Version:** 1.1 **Authority:** Reference **Status:** Living

## Purpose

Defines official project terminology. Definitions only.

## Terms

**Accelerated Biology** --- The defining biological condition of the
Wilds.

**Canon** --- Officially established project information.

**Chlora** --- The project's protagonist.

**Custodians** --- The caretakers of the Skyhook.

**Dormant State** --- The inactive condition of the juvenile symbiont.

**Edge** --- The environmental transition surrounding the Wilds.

**Frozen** --- The long-lived leadership of Skyhook civilization.

**Outlier** --- The transition region between the Skyhook and the Wilds.

**Pickers** --- Recovery specialists operating near the Edge.

**Skyhook** --- Humanity's orbital mechanical structure and primary
settlement.

**Symbiont** --- A living organism capable of biological partnership
with a host.

**Wilds** --- The ancient biological environment beyond the Edge.

## Naming Convention

Use: - The Edge - The Wilds - The Outlier - Skyhook - Symbiont -
Frozen - Custodians - Pickers
