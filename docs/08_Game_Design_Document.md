# Vision Statement

Chlora is a story-driven exploration JRPG inspired by the readability
and charm of classic 16-bit games while emphasizing ecology, curiosity,
and scientific discovery.

Knowledge---not combat---is the primary form of progression.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Gameplay Pillars

## Explore

Discover new regions of the Skyhook, the Outlier, and the Wilds through
observation and experimentation.

## Understand

Learn how organisms, environments, and systems function. Understanding
unlocks new opportunities.

## Adapt

The symbiont expands Chlora's ability to safely interact with the Wilds
through biological partnership.

## Connect

Relationships with people, organisms, and ecosystems drive both
progression and narrative.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Gameplay Loop

1.  Explore a new area.
2.  Observe organisms and environmental patterns.
3.  Learn how the ecosystem functions.
4.  Gain new ecological capabilities through the symbiont.
5.  Revisit earlier locations with new understanding.
6.  Discover additional paths, story, and species.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Exploration Philosophy

Players should be rewarded for:

-   Curiosity
-   Observation
-   Experimentation
-   Pattern recognition
-   Environmental awareness

The desired question is:

> "What happens if I interact with this?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Symbiont Progression

The symbiont develops through ecological interaction.

Potential areas of growth include:

-   Environmental sensing
-   Communication
-   Camouflage
-   Pollination
-   Environmental adaptation
-   Biological translation

Its primary role is ecological rather than martial.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Combat Philosophy

Conflict exists, but it is not the primary focus.

Whenever appropriate, encounters should reward understanding, avoidance,
adaptation, or cooperation rather than simple elimination.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Art Direction

Gameplay presentation targets:

-   Chrono Trigger--style sprite readability
-   Painterly concept art
-   Clear silhouettes
-   Expressive environmental animation
