# CHLORA PROJECT

# 14 --- Production Roadmap

**Version:** 1.0\
**Status:** Living Document\
**Tier:** Support

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Purpose

The Production Roadmap tracks the development of the Chlora project from
concept through release.

Unlike the Design Journal, which explains past decisions, this document
focuses on future work. It should be updated regularly as priorities
change.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Workflow

Tasks move through these stages:

-   Backlog
-   Ready
-   In Progress
-   Review
-   Complete

Only move a task when its acceptance criteria have been met.
