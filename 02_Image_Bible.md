## Approved Visual Standards

### IMG-001 Chlora

-   Short bob haircut.
-   Practical explorer clothing.
-   Hair behaves naturally until symbiont activation.
-   Symbiont appears living, never mechanical armor.

### IMG-002 Skyhook

-   Entirely mechanical.
-   Aerospace engineering.
-   Functional maintenance-first design.

### IMG-003 Wilds

-   Macro biology.
-   Dense ecological layering.
-   Soft particulate atmosphere.
-   Inspired by, but not identical to, Earth organisms.
